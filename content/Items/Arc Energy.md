A mysterious energy generated within the perpetual [[Arc Storm]]. Its basic effects are similar to that of conventional lightning, emitting a purplish-white light when travelling through air and when stored and channeled in [[#Aetherium]].

By manipulating the geometric arrangement of the energy, the energy can be made to affect its environment in strange ways. This is usually done by carving Aetherium cores into carefully designed patters. At its most basic, this resembles a runic system, but its rapid development has reached the point where the complexity of the patterns approaches that of modern day electronic processors.

It is used as a source of energy for the many devices that are developed and produced within [[Hyperius]]. 
# Aetherium
Formed exclusively within [[Arc Tree]]s' cores, Aetherium is the only known way to store and and control the effects of Arc Energy.

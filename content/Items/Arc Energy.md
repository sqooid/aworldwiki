A mysterious energy generated within the perpetual [[Arc Storm]]. Its basic effects are similar to that of conventional lightning, emitting a purplish-white light when travelling through air and when stored within an [[Arc Trees#Aetherium Core]].

By manipulating the geometric arrangement of the energy, the energy can be made to affect its environment in various ways. This is usually done by carving 

It is used as a source of energy for the many devices that are developed and produced within [[Hyperius]].
